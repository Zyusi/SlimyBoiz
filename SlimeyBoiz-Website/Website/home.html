About

TThis is a score-based party 2D platforming game. A key feature of the project is the asymmetric gameplay which revolves around one player holding an tablet with the aim to hinder the other players who are playing on a console. The gameplay is a movement based 2D platformer where, if the players do not progress through the game in time, they will be eliminated, unable to gain more points and will be marked as the next tablet player for the following control change over. As the tablet player, the goal is to eliminate at least one player so that you can be exchanged for that player and enabled to gain points again.

The players are split into two teams, one with a single player that holds the tablet and the rest with controllers that are on the playing field. The goal of the players with the controllers is to maintain speed while collecting points if they lose speed they will be killed. At first, the game is presented as a free for all for the players that are on the map, however, they have options for team play for people that work together as a team. They must do this while the tablet play who is not a physical being on the field of play but more of a god-like character attempts to slow them down enough that one of is hit by the grinder. At which point if the player has had the tablet for the set time limit the tablet player switches with the one that died and is then able to start gaining points again. The winner of the game is the player or the team with the most points at the end of the game.
